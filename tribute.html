Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao was born in a Hindu agrarian family on 28 May 1923[1] in Nimmakuru, a small village in Gudivada taluk of Krishna district, which was a part of the erstwhile Madras Presidency of British India.
